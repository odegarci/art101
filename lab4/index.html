Making Black Milk Tea with Boba

  1. Gather all ingredients: 1/2 lbs bag of dry tapioca pearls, 1/2 lbs bag of loose leaf black tea, brown sugar, 1/4 lbs powder creamer, and water
  2. Boil an entire pot of water at medium heat
  3. Once it starts boiling, add your bag of dried tapioca pearls
  4. Stir the boba pearls every 10 minutes for 30 minutes to make sure the boba balls don't stick to the bottom of the pot
  5. Once the 30 minutes are up, pour the pot over a large strainer and rinse the boba balls with cold water
  6. In a separate large container, add 1.5 cups of cold water and a one cup of brown sugar. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  7. Add the boba pearls into the container with the brown sugar syrup we made. Stir to incorporate into the balls.
  8. Set aside the boba pearls. In another large pot, fill 3/4 of the pot with water and let it boil.
  9. Once the water starts to boil, add entire bag of loose black tea leaves into pot
  10. Cover the pot with the lid and make sure the heat is on a medium setting. Wait 10 minutes.
  11. After 10 minutes turn off the heat, let it set for 5 more minutes.
  12. Scoop out the tea leaves as soon as 5 minutes are up to prevent tea from turning bitter.
  13. Add ice until contents in the pot reach the top. Stir ice so it melts inside tea.
  14. Pour the tea into a separate larger pot.
  15. Add 24 oz of powder creamer into tea and stir thoroughly
  16. Add one scoop of boba pearls from our mixture into a cup. Then add desired amount of ice into it.
  17. Add desired amount of tea into cup followed by desired amount of sugar into it.
  18. Stir drink until fully incorporated
  19. Add a fat boba straw into drink and sip.
